Game: 
Parcel Panic

Instructions:
Move using arrow keys or WASD.
Press space near a box on the conveyor belt to pick it up.
Press space again to drop it. You can place it on another conveyor belt or on the blue square to trigger a minigame.
Hover your mouse over the arrow on the right side of the screen to open the ticketing system.
The timer is displayed in the top left corner.

Cheats:
Alt - Enter : Add 30 seconds to timer
Alt - X : Reset timer to 3 minutes
Alt - P: Pause and Unpause timer
